The Olive Center is located in the Sensory Building of the Robert Mondavi Institute for Wine and Food Science, adjacent to Interstate 80.

  1. Take Interstate 80 to the UC Davis exit.
  2. Turn north at the offramp.
  3. Drive toward the orange stucco buildings on the right side of the road, beyond the vineyard (the Olive Center is located in the first building that you will pass, in the Robert Mondavi Institute for Wine and Food Science.)
  4. Go straight through the traffic circle and turn right on Hilgard Lane.
  5. Park in the lot straight ahead.  There is a machine to pay a parking permit fee of $9.  Display the parking permit on your dashboard.
  6. The Olive Center is located in the Sensory Building, Room 1021.
