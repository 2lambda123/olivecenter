April 12, 2023

Hosted by Kiler Ridge Olive Farm

You're invited to an in-person gathering in the Central Coast to meet COOC staff and directors!
Who's Invited: COOC members and California olive oil producers, wholesalers, and retailers interested in learning more about the COOC.
Purpose: To give COOC staff, directors, members, and potential members an opportunity to meet in person and share information.
When: April 12, 2023, 4 p.m. to 7 p.m.
Where: Kiler Ridge Olive Farm 1111 Kiler Canyon Rd. Paso Robles, CA 93446
RSVP: by April 7, 2023 to eoliver@calapple.org

If the Central Coast meeting is not convenient, stay tuned! Plans are underway for meetings in other California olive oil producing regions.
