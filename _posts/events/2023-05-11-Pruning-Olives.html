Saturday May 13, 2023

The UC Davis Olive Center invites you to our second workshop of 2023 in the Spanish education series. This workshop is designed for Spanish speaking farm managers, agronomists, farm workers and field personnel. The Workshop will cover the basic principles of pruning olive trees and the different techniques used for multiple production systems. This event is brought to you thanks to the collaboration of Cobram Estate and Infaco USA.

For more information, please contact Adele Amico Roxas at adamicoroxas@ucdavis.edu
